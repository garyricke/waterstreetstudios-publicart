About the Initiative

Art beyond the gallery walls

The Batavia Public Art Initiative is a sub-committee of Water Street Studios, dedicated to immersing art within the community. We believe art should live where people live — on the riverwalk, along downtown corridors, and in the spaces where neighbors gather.

Community Connection

Establish a sense of community in Batavia and the greater Fox Valley through outdoor art installations that celebrate diverse local voices.

Public Awareness

Increase public awareness, appreciation, and contribution of public art — making creativity accessible and visible to everyone.

Place & Nature

Connect people to the beauty, diversity, and importance of the Fox River through art that celebrates our natural surroundings.

Civic Identity

Build community pride, honor Batavia's past, and inspire a shared sense of belonging and responsibility for the future.

Get Involved

Paint a piece of your city

Every mural we create is painted by the community, for the community. No experience required — just good energy and a willingness to pick up a brush.

Paint Days

Join AM or PM shifts during mural installations. Help fill in designs, clean brushes, and be part of something lasting. Ages 14+ welcome. Weekend slots available for students.

Committee Roles

Serve on the BPAI committee. Roles include fundraising, site logistics, marketing, press writing, outreach coordination, and event planning. Two-year terms.
